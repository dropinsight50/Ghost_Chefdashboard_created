Frequently Asked Questions

You can cancel a booking from the "My Bookings" page. Click on the booking you wish to cancel and select the "Cancel" button. Please note that cancellation policies apply: more than 48 hours before booking starts, you'll receive a full refund; 24-48 hours before, you'll receive a 50% refund; less than 24 hours before, no refund will be provided.

If a kitchen owner cancels your booking, you'll receive a full refund regardless of when the cancellation occurs. We'll also help you find an alternative kitchen space if needed and may provide a discount coupon for your inconvenience.

If you encounter any issues with a kitchen, please report it to us within 24 hours of your booking by visiting the "My Bookings" page, selecting the relevant booking, and clicking "Report Issue." Provide details and any photos of the problem. Our support team will investigate and help resolve the situation.

Most kitchens allow bookings up to 3 months in advance, though some may permit bookings up to 6 months ahead. The available booking window is displayed on each kitchen's listing page. Last-minute bookings are also possible if there's availability, though some kitchens require a minimum notice period (typically 24-48 hours).

All kitchens on our platform are required to meet local health and safety regulations. Kitchen owners must provide documentation of health department certifications and regular inspections. We also conduct periodic quality checks and rely on user reviews to ensure ongoing compliance with safety and cleanliness standards.
